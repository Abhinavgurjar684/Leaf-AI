    Farmer Tips

    Smart Farming Advice

    Expert tips to keep your crops healthy all season long

    Proper Watering

    Water crops early morning to reduce evaporation. Check soil moisture 2 inches deep before watering again.

    Nutrient Balance

    Yellow leaves often indicate nitrogen deficiency. Use organic compost or balanced NPK fertilizer regularly.

    Pest Prevention

    Inspect crops every 3 days. Remove infected leaves immediately. Use neem oil spray as natural pesticide.

    Weather Monitoring

    Extreme heat or cold can stress plants. Use shade nets during peak summer and cover crops during frost.

    Pruning Technique

    Remove dead or diseased branches with clean, sharp tools. This improves airflow and prevents disease spread.

    Organic Farming

    Use vermicompost, green manure, and bio-pesticides to maintain healthy soil microbiome and strong plants.
